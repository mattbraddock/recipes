French Toast

Ingredients

Roux

  • 50 grams all-purpose flour
  • 75 grams water
  • 75 grams milk

Dough

  • 150 grams warm water
  • 35 grams granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 250 grams all-purpose flour
  • 200 grams semolina flour
  • 25 grams dry milk powder
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1 tbs honey
  • 1 egg & 2 egg yolks
  • 4 tbs unsalted butter, softened

Wash

  • 2 egg whites
  • 1 tbs milk

Bath

  • 2 cups half and half
  • 6 eggs
  • 4 tbs honey, warmed
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

Bread

  • Make roux (bring liquid to a boil, pour over flour, mix) and cool
  • Combine water, sugar, and yeast
  • Mix ingredients sans butter in a mixer until flour is hydrated
  • Add butter in at least 2 stages
  • Mix with dough hook until dough sticks less on walls of the bowl
  • Shape dough into a ball, then cover and rest for 60 minutes
  • Perform a fold of the dough, then cover and rest for 45 minutes
  • Separate the dough into 3 equal pieces, then roll and braid the dough
  • Place in a greased loaf pan, then cover and rest for 90 minutes
  • Apply wash, then bake at 350F for 35 minutes and transfer to a cooling rack when done

French Toast

  • Slice the bread into thick slices
  • Mix all the bath ingredients with a whisk
  • For each slice of bread, soak both sides for 2-3 minutes each, then immediately transfer to a hot griddle and cook to desired finish
