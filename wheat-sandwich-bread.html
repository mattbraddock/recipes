Wheat Sandwich Bread

Ingredients

Preferment

  • 200 grams bread flour
  • 125 grams water
  • 1/8 tsp instant yeast

Dough

  • 200 grams white whole wheat flour
  • 100 grams bread flour
  • 30 grams granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 225 grams whole milk
  • 2 tbs unsalted butter, softened

Directions

  • Mix together the preferment, cover, and refrigerate overnight (at least 6 hours)
    • Set out butter to soften during this time
  • Remove the preferment from the refrigerate and allow to come to room temperature, at least 1 hour
  • Whisk together the flour, sugar, and salt, then add in the yeast
  • Add in the milk and combine until fully hydrated
  • Combine with the preferment and knead until gluten is well developed
  • Add the butter and knead until smooth
  • Place in a lightly greased bowl, then cover and rest for 35 minutes
  • Gently deflate the dough before folding, then cover and rest for 35 minutes
  • Move the dough to a clean surface and allow it to rest for 10 minutes
  • Shape the dough and place in a lightly greased bread pan, then cover and rest for 45 minutes
    • Preheat oven to 220F while the dough rests
  • Place the pan into the oven and immediately turn the oven up to 400F
  • Bake for 35 minutes, covering with foil after approximately 25 minutes if it begins browning quickly
  • Move to a cooling rack and allow to cool completely before slicing
