Wheat Sandwich Bread

Ingredients

Preferment

  • 200 grams bread flour
  • 125 grams water
  • 1/8 tsp instant yeast

Roux

  • 75 grams bread flour
  • 125 grams boiling water

Dough

  • 125 grams whole milk
  • 225 grams white whole wheat flour
  • 30 grams granulated sugar
  • 2 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt
  • 3/4 tsp dry malt powder
  • 2 tbs unsalted butter, softened

Directions

  • Mix together the preferment, then cover and rest for 6-10 hours
  • Mix together the roux, then cover and refrigerate for 6-10 hours
    • Set out butter to soften during this time
  • Remove the roux from the refrigerator and allow to come to room temperature, approximately 1 hour
  • Combine the preferment, roux, and milk in a stand mixer and mix until loosened up
  • Whisk together the dry ingredients, add to the stand mixer, and mix until well integrated and dough does not stick to the sides of the bowl
  • Add the butter and knead until smooth
  • Shape into a ball and place in a lightly greased bowl, then cover and rest for 30 minutes
  • Gently deflate the dough before folding, then cover and rest for 30 minutes
  • Move the dough to a clean surface and allow it to rest for 5 minutes
  • Shape the dough and place in a lightly greased bread pan, then cover and rest for 30 minutes
    • Preheat oven to 220F while the dough rests
  • Place the pan into the oven and immediately turn the oven up to 400F
  • Bake for 20 minutes, then cover with foil and continue baking for an addition 15 minutes
  • Move to a cooling rack and allow to cool completely before slicing

Notes

  • While the initial combining of preferment, roux, and remaining dough ingredients is best done with a stand mixer, all remaining kneading can be done by hand
