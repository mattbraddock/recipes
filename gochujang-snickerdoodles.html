Gochujang Snickerdoodles

Ingredients

  • 1 tbs & 7 tbs unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 tbs dark brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbs gochujang
  • 3/4 tsp honey
  • 1/2 tbs light brown sugar
  • 200 grams granulated sugar
  • 1/2 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 egg & 1 egg yolk
  • 210 grams all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder

Directions

  • Mix together 1 tbs butter, dark brown sugar, gochujang, and honey; set aside
  • Mix together 7 tbs butter, light brown sugar, granulated sugar, salt, cinnamon, vanilla, and eggs
  • Add in flour and baking powder, mixing until well combined
  • Microwave the gochujang mixture for 5 seconds, then swirl into dough
  • Start to preheat oven to 350F and refrigerate dough as oven preheats
  • Once preheated, place dough on lined baking sheets using a medium scoop
  • Bake cookies for approximately 10 minutes, then move to cooling racks

Notes

  • 200 grams of sugar is approximately 1 cup
  • 210 grams of all-purpose flour is approximately 1 3/4 cups
