Banana (Nut) Bread

Ingredients

  • 8 tbs unsalted butter, softened
  • 1/3 cup light brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 tsp ground nutmeg
  • 340 grams bananas, mashed
  • 3 tbs seedless raspberry fruit spread
  • 1 tbs honey
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 eggs
  • 125 grams all-purpose flour
  • 150 grams cake flour
  • 1/3 cup walnuts, chopped (optional)

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 325F
  • Combine the butter, brown sugar, baking powder, baking soda, salt, cinnamon, and nutmeg using a stand mixer
  • Add in bananas, fruit spread, honey, vanilla, and eggs, then beat until well mixed
  • Add in flour and walnuts, if including, and stir until smooth
  • Spoon into a lightly greased loaf pan, smooth out the top, and rest for 10 minutes
  • Bake for 45 minutes, then cover with foil and bake for 25 additional minutes
  • Allow to cool for 10 minutes in pan before moving to a cooling rack

Notes

  • 340 grams of bananas is approximately 3-4 bananas
