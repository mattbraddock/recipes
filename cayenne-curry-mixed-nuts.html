Cayenne & Curry Mixed Nuts

Ingredients

  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp paprika
  • 1 tsp habanero salt
  • 1 tsp ground cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 tsp red curry powder
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp ground ginger
  • 1/2 tsp ground cinnamon
  • 1/2 tsp ground cardamom
  • 1/4 tsp turmeric
  • 1/4 cup olive oil
  • 4 cups mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts, etc.)

Directions

  • Preheat oven to 325F
  • Combine all spices, then add oil and stir until well combined
  • Add nuts and toss/stir until evenly coated
  • Spread the nuts on lined baking sheets and bake for 13-15 minutes
  • Allow the nuts to cool for an hour before storing in an airtight container
