Ciabatta

Ingredients

Preferment

  • 200 grams bread flour
  • 150 grams water
  • 1/8 tsp instant yeast

Dough

  • 300 grams bread flour
  • 225 grams lukewarm water
  • 25 grams extra virgin olive oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 tsp salt

Directions

  • Mix together the flour, water, and yeast for the preferment until the flour is hydrated, then cover and rest for 12-16 hours
  • After the resting period, mix together the preferment and remaining dough ingredients until a shaggy mess
  • Turn the dough out onto a clean surface and knead using the stretch and fold technique until gluten is well developed, up to 15 minutes of constant work
  • Form the dough into a ball, place in a bowl, then cover and rest for 45 minutes
  • Turn the dough out onto a lightly floured surface and perform a fold, then cover and rest for 45 minutes
  • Repeat the previous step
  • Divide the dough equally into as many pieces as desired (2 to 6 recommended), gently shape into rectangles, then cover and rest for 45 minutes
    • As the resting period begins, preheat oven to 475F and put a baking stone inside
  • Transfer the dough to parchment paper dusted with semolina, and transfer them to the baking stone in the oven
  • Immediately spray the dough and inside the oven with a fine mist of water and immediately shut the oven door
  • Bake until the loaves are puffy and golden brown (approximately 15-25 minutes depending on size of loaves), then transfer to a cooling rack
